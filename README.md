1. Introduction
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of financial performance across major Indian companies. It visualizes market capitalization, quarterly sales, and company segmentation to help stakeholders understand financial distribution and identify top performers.

2. Business Problem
Financial analysts and decision-makers often struggle to compare companies across multiple dimensions like market cap, sales volume, and category classification. Without a centralized view, it's difficult to identify trends, outliers, and investment opportunities.

3. Goal of the Dashboard
- Segment companies by Market Cap Category and Sales Category
- Identify top-performing companies in terms of Market Capitalization and Quarterly Sales
- Visualize financial distribution across categories for strategic insights
- Enable interactive filtering for dynamic analysis

5. Highlighted Insights
- Large Cap companies dominate the market with ~78% share.
- Reliance Industries and TCS lead in both market cap and sales.
- High Sales category includes energy and automotive giants like IOCL and Tata Motors.
- Mid and Small Cap companies show lower sales but potential for growth.

6. Business Impact
- Investor Strategy: Helps identify stable large-cap investments and high-growth mid-cap opportunities.
- Sales Benchmarking: Enables comparison across industries and sales categories.
- Market Segmentation: Supports strategic planning for portfolio diversification.
- Decision Support: Empowers financial analysts with interactive, real-time insights.
